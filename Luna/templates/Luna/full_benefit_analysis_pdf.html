Definitions
  • Savings: The difference between your Bill w/Out Solar* and your Bill w/Solar.
  • Vivint Solar Bill: Your Vivint Solar Bill based on the Actual Production of the solar energy system at your home and your contract rate during that month.
  • Utility Bill: The subtotal of your charges for energy and connection to the grid.
  • Bill w/Solar: The sum of your Utility Bill and your Vivint Solar Bill.
  • Bill w/out Solar*: Your Total Consumption multiplied by your Average Utility Rate.
  • Actual Production: The amount of energy produced by the solar energy system at your hom.
  • Consumption from Panels: The amount of energy your home used that was produced by the solar energy system.
  • Excess Production Sent to Grid: The amount of energy registered by your utility's net meter.
  • Consumption from Utility: The amount of energy your home drew from the grid.
  • Total Consumption: The total amount of energy you needed to meet your usage habits.
Footnote * Values are calculated using your Total Consumption and Average Utility Rate.
